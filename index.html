One of my favotites poem

                    The Path Ahead



                    A journey starts with steps so small,
                    
                    Yet each one leads, despite the fall.
                    
                    Through winding roads and skies so wide,
                    
                    The path unfolds with time as guide.
                    
                    
                    
                    The weight you bear, the dreams you chase,
                    
                    Are markers of your strength and grace.
                    
                    Through trials fierce and winds that wail,
                    
                    You rise again you will not fail.
                    
                    
                    
                    For in your heart, a fire glows,
                    
                    A light that only courage knows.
                    
                    Keep walking on, embrace the fight,
                    
                    The dawn will break you'll find your light.
